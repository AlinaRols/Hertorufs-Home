Product details.

High-performance textured artificial turf track with integrated markings and numbering — ideal for functional training, movement drills and sled work.

  • High fibre density: 86,000 stitches/m² for exceptional grip and comfort on high-intensity floor work.
  • Excellent impact resistance, built for heavy daily use in gyms.
  • Easy to clean and maintain, with quick, simple installation.
  • Available in several sizes — and we also make custom tracks to fit your exact project.
  • Made in the EU in a facility with over 100 years of textile experience in technical carpets and mats.
